About The Department

Department of Information Technology has played a key role in shaping the technological landscape of our college since 1997, by empowering students, faculty, and staff with cutting-edge solutions and fostering a digitally driven learning environment. At present, the total sanctioned intake of the IT department is 240, which is delegated by the well-knit and qualified faculty demonstrating high professionalism which helps the graduating engineers to attain excellence in all walks of life.

The department offers a comprehensive curriculum covering both foundation and advanced aspects of Information practices, keeping pace with the rapidly evolving field. The department has excellent infrastructure and lab facilities which are upgraded from time to time to provide good practical learning and innovative environment for the students and researchers. The department of IT takes pride in a strong alumni association, spanning the globe, adding significant value to our community. Aspiring IT talents thrive in global placements, fueled by enormous career growth opportunities.

About Pixel O Hack

Embark on a thrilling journey into the realm of technology and innovation in our upcoming event, "Pixel O Hack," organized by the Information Technology Department. This convergence promises to showcase cutting-edge developments that define the digital landscape, creating an immersive experience charged with the excitement of technological discovery.

The extraordinary learning experience is anchored based on two main themes. The theme "Looming White Haters" offers a networking-based opportunity for students to collaborate and gain hands-on experience in Networking Concepts and Cyber Security basics. Actively engaging with the latest tools and technologies will foster a practical understanding of the subject.Under the theme "Graphics with Gravitas," students can explore and learn activities like Poster Making, Photoshop, Video Editing, and Animations, utilizing a variety of Graphic Design Services. This theme aims to enhance creative skills and emphasize the importance of visual communication.
